Target Audience

Target Audience Parameters

Our target audience ranges between mid 30's thru late 80's. The main purpose for there visits are for accurate weather travel information, bad enough weather that power outages might happen. It is critical that they recievce the most reliable information. That is why they visit this site becasue of a high level of accuracy.

Persona's

First persona, this person wants to know weather conditions for travel. Are the roads icy or not. Age 44 male has concerns taking his family out on the road during bad weather.

Second persona this person is age 80 and is concerned about power outages due to bad weather. It is important to this person to have accuracy. The reason is so they can plan ahead with ther family what they might do in an emergency situation.
